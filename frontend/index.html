Our Story

Founded in 2020, Chai Theory began with a simple mission: to bring the authentic taste of Indian chai to modern café culture. Our journey started in a small kitchen in Mumbai, experimenting with traditional spice blends and contemporary brewing techniques.

Today, we pride ourselves on serving not just beverages, but experiences. Each cup of chai is crafted with carefully sourced ingredients and prepared with the perfect balance of tradition and innovation.
